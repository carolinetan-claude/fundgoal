Problem Statement

Billions watch the World Cup.
Most feel like spectators in life, not just sport.

🌍 The Disconnection Problem

A large share of the global population feels disconnected from major events like the World Cup — they miss out on moments of genuine connection and excitement that unite billions.

😔 The Passivity Problem

Even those who watch feel like passive consumers. There's no way to participate meaningfully — no stake, no impact, no reason to care beyond the final whistle.

💰 The Impact Gap

FIFA generated $7.5B in 2022. Almost none went back to the communities that produce the talent. Fans who want to give have no transparent, direct way to do it.
